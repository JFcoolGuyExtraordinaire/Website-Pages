Who Are We?

MAXXPower, a family company, is the largest power company in the state of Virginia. We have our roots in the greater Richmond area and have held true to our values of providing the safest energy experience for every home.

Making Energy

At MAXXPower we utilize sources of energy that naturally replenish in order to keep the Richmond area cleaner and healthier. These renewable energy sources are virtually endless!


Moving Energy

There are hundreds of miles of MAXXPower transmission lines connecting every home in Virginia to the energy they need. We strive to constantly maintain and improve transmission and respond to outages as soon as possible.


Values and Commitments

At MAXXPower we value safety most. We work to protect natural resources and keep staff and consumers safe through using clean and efficient energy.

Projects

At MAXXPower we are determined to continue improving service for our customers. We are analyzing regions prone to outages and implementing underground lines, as well building high-voltage transmission to improve grid reliability.
